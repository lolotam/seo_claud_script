<p>The history of <strong>4711 Cologne</strong> dates back to 1792 when an Italian perfumer created this revolutionary formula in Cologne, Germany. The name "4711" comes from the building's address where it was first produced. During the French occupation of Cologne, all houses were numbered, and the building received the number 4711. Today, this <a href="https://xsellpoint.com/product-category/best-sellers/">best-selling fragrance</a> continues to be produced according to the original secret formula.</p>
